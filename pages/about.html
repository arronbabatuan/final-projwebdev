About Us

Pollyanne's Crispy Lechon is a family run business based
in Poblacion-Camolinas-Housing,Cordova,Cebu. Proudly
serving authentic, mouthwatering lechon that's crispy on the
outside and tender on the inside. Founded by Carla Wahing
and Joel Wahing, we use a time-tested family recipe and roast
each lechon to golden perfection—perfect for any celebration.
